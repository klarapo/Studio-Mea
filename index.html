ABOUT

How we came to be.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It all started with an idea while lounging in a coffee shop. After a while of brainstorming, we came to conclusion of "why not try?". We started researching how to open a business, contacting old colegues for advice and gathering all the knowledge we found out.

  • March 2011

    Our Studio is Born

    After a lot of planing and researching, our Studio was born and the doors of our office were finally open for business.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    After much learning and some freelance work, we became a full service where clients came to us and recognized our work.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    Success was following us over the years of work and there was the demand to expand. We hired new developers and opened another office.
